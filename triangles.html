Triangles Rewards

Design Problem

Aim to offer a new way to earn with the Canadian Tire Loyalty program, Triangle Rewards App, allowing members to earn Triangles — rewards points, and use those points to redeem offers, in an effort to attract potential customers and a younger crowd.

Final Proposal
